Privacy Policy — Effective Date: April 14, 2026

1. Overview

FocusBeast ("we", "us", "our") built the FocusBeast app as a commercial product. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information.

  • No account required. FocusBeast works entirely offline with no sign-up.
  • No analytics. We do not use Firebase, Google Analytics, Amplitude, or any third-party tracking SDK.
  • No ads. We do not serve advertisements.
  • All data stays on your device. Your tasks, streaks, XP, and preferences are stored locally using AsyncStorage and never leave your device.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Contacts or calendar
  • Browsing history or app usage data

4. Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, FocusBeast schedules a local notification on your device at 9:00 AM. This notification is handled entirely by your operating system. No data is sent to our servers. We have no servers.

5. Premium & Payments

FocusBeast offers an in-app purchase for Premium features. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android) through their respective stores. We never see, store, or process your payment information.

  • Apple: Managed through your Apple ID and App Store subscription
  • Google: Managed through your Google Play subscription

6. Data Retention

All data is stored on your device only. Uninstalling the app deletes all data permanently. We have no ability to recover it.

7. Children's Privacy

FocusBeast does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, our service is safe for all ages.

8. Third-Party Services

FocusBeast does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect personal data. Our only dependencies are:

  • Expo / React Native (app framework)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store (distribution and payments)

9. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your device. We recommend using a passcode or biometric lock on your device.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
