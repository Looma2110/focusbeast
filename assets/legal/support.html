Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add tasks?

Tap the "+" button on the Home screen. You can add up to 3 tasks per day. Choose a category (work, sport, craft, study, or yoga) to see your beast in the matching mood.

How do I complete a task?

Tap the checkbox next to any task. If you accidentally complete a task, tap it again to undo. You earn 10 XP per task completed.

What are subtasks?

Tap "Split" on any task to break it into smaller steps. Each subtask gives 5 XP. Complete all subtasks to automatically mark the parent task as done.

How does the focus timer work?

Go to the Focus tab, choose a duration (15, 25, or 45 minutes), and start. Your beast will accompany you. Complete the session to earn bonus XP. You get one free session, then Premium is required for unlimited sessions.

How do streaks work?

Complete at least one task per day to maintain your streak. You get 1 grace day per week — if you miss a day, your streak is protected once. After that, missing a day resets your streak to 0.

How do I unlock new beasts?

Earn XP by completing tasks and focus sessions. New beasts unlock at XP milestones: Cat (100 XP), Monkey (300 XP, Premium), Rabbit (600 XP, Premium), Fox (1200 XP, Premium), Lion (2400 XP, Premium).

How do I rename my beast?

Go to Settings, tap on your active beast to rename it.

I lost all my data! What happened?

FocusBeast stores all data locally on your device. If you uninstalled the app or reset your device, your data is permanently deleted. We don't have servers, so we cannot recover it. We recommend not uninstalling the app.

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions (iOS) or Google Play Store > Subscriptions (Android). Cancel at least 24 hours before renewal to avoid being charged.

The app is buggy or crashed. What should I do?

Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, contact us at support@focusbeast.app with a description of the problem and your device model.
